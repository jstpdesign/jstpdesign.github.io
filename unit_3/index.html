Hi, I'm Jay St. Pierre

I am a newspaper designer, currently living in Minneapolis, Minn. I work for the Star Tribune, where I serve as the lead designer and art director for the sports section. Scroll down to learn more about me and see some of my work.

Hey, that's me! (there will be a pic of me to the left)

Born and raised in Destrehan, La. (that's right outside of New Orleans for those who are like "Where the heck is Destrehan?!?"), I have worked for a handful of newspapers since attending LSU. I started my career in 2009 as a news designer at the Clarion-Ledger in Jackson, Miss.

From there, I moved to Colorado Springs where I became the lead sports designer at the The Gazette. I held the same position at The World-Herald in Omaha, Neb. I picked up tabloid and digital design experience when I worked at RedEye in Chicago before making the bold choice to head north to Minneapolis.

When I'm not designing sports, I'm usually watching them. Football is my sport of choice, but I also enjoy baseball in the summer and basketball in the winter. I'm still learning to love hockey, which is becoming increasingly easier the longer I live up North.

My other pastimes include watching movies and cooking for my friends and coworkers. As the only Cajun in Minneapolis (please donâ€™t fact check that), I enjoy bringing a slice of home to the Twin Cities.
