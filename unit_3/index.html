Hi, I'm Jay St. Pierre

I am a newspaper designer, currently living in Minneapolis, Minn. I work for the Star Tribune, where I serve as the publication's lead sports designer. Scroll down to learn more about me and see some of my work.

Hey, that's me! (there will be a pic of me to the left)

Born and raised in Destrehan, La. (that's right outside of New Orleans for those who are like "Where the heck is Destrehan?!?"), I have worked for a handful of newspapers since attending LSU. I started my career as a news designer at the Clarion-Ledger in Jackson, Miss. From there, I moved to Colorado Springs where I became the lead sports designer at the The Gazette. I held the same position at The World-Herald in Omaha, Neb. I picked up tabloid and digital design experience when I worked at RedEye in Chicago and then made the bold choice to head north to Minneapolis.
